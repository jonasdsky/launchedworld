FAQ
Questions answered.
Who can apply to Launched? +
Current students in their final year and recent graduates within 2 years from any university. We evaluate on merit — a motivated grad from Baruch gets the same treatment as one from Harvard.
Is it really free? +
Yes, completely free for students. We're paid by the companies when we successfully match and connect a graduate. You never pay anything.
What majors do you work with? +
All of them. Engineering and CS students go into technical roles. Business, economics, and liberal arts students go into GTM, operations, and strategy roles. These companies need all types — they just need people who are exceptional and mission-aligned.
How long does the process take? +
Average time from application to signed offer is 3 weeks. Some happen in days. The AI interview is 15 minutes, matches are usually delivered within a week, and the companies we work with move fast.
What if I already have a job offer? +
Apply anyway. It takes 10 minutes and the roles we're matching you with may be significantly better. We'd rather you compare than miss out.
How does the AI interview work? +
A 15-minute video conversation that asks about your interests, values, how you think, and what kind of work you want to do. It's not a screening — it's a discovery. The goal is to understand you better than any resume could.
Do you work with non-target schools? +
That's a core part of our mission. The companies we work with can't get enough talent from target schools — they need people who are hungry, capable, and motivated regardless of pedigree. Our AI assessment finds those people wherever they are.
